Speech Denoising Headset

The goal of this project is to develop a real-time, end-to-end speech denoising headset. The headset is able to denoise noisy speech signals in real-time using a microphone array positioned around the headset. The microphone array performs a mixture of multi-channel and single-channel speech enhancement algorithms.

Collaborators

This work is being done under the supervision of Prof. Bhaskar Rao and Prof. Truong Nguyen, with funding provided by Korea Electronics Technology Institute. The project is a collaboration between myself, Ritwik Giri, Dr. Harinath Garudadri, Chinghua Lee, Alican Nalci, and Nasim Radmanesh.
