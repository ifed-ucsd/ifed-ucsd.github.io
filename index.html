Igor Fedorov

I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California at San Diego. I am advised by Prof. Bhaskar Rao and co-advised by Prof. Truong Nguyen. I am a member of the DSP Lab and Video Processing Lab.

I received the B.S. and M.S. degrees in Electrical Engineering in 2012 and 2014, respectively, from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, advised by Prof. Pierre Moulin.

My research interests include

  • Machine learning
  • Multimodal data fusion
  • Dictionary learning
  • Sparse recovery
  • Signal processing
  • Deep learning

I am lucky to be married to Katarina Fedorov.
