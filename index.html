Igor Fedorov

I am a Staff Research Engineer in the machine learning research group at ARM Research. I completed my PhD in Electrical Engineering in 2018 at the University of California San-Diego. I was supervised by Prof. Bhaskar Rao and co-advised by Prof. Truong Nguyen. Before UCSD, I completed my B.S. and M.S. (supervised by Prof. Pierre Moulin) in Electrical Engineering at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

I am lucky to be married to Katarina Fedorov.
