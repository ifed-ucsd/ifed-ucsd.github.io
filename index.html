Igor Fedorov

I am a PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California at San Diego. I am advised by Prof. Bhaskar Rao and Prof. Truong Nguyen. I am a member of the Video Processing Lab and DSP Lab.

I finished my B.S. and M.S. in Electrical Engineering in 2012 and 2014 at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (advised by Prof. Pierre Moulin).

My research interests include

  • Machine learning
  • Multimodal data fusion
  • Dictionary learning
  • Sparse recovery
  • Signal processing
