Igor Fedorov

Hello! My name is Igor Fedorov. I am a PhD student in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of California at San Diego. I am advised by Prof. Truong Nguyen and Prof. Bhaskar Rao. I am a member of the Video Processing Lab and DSP Lab.

I finished my B.S. and M.S. in Electrical Engineering in 2012 and 2014 at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (advised by Prof. Pierre Moulin).

My research interests include machine learning, compressed sensing, and signal processing. I am interested in applying these topics in the context of speech and video signals as well as in multi-modal systems.
